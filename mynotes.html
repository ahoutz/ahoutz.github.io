My Notes

Below are notes/derivations I have handwritten. As such, I cannot promise their accuracy nor legibility. Some people find handwritten notes easier to follow than typed notes though, so I decided to provide them here.

Canonical New Keynesian Model Derivation with NGDP Targeting Rule

I derive the canonical three equation New Keynesian model with an NGDP targeting rule from microfoundations. I go on to solve the model analytically and derive propositions 1 and 2 and lemma 1 in my paper A Systematic Analysis of NGDP Targeting.

Medium Scale New Keynesian Model

I derive a medium scale New Keynesian model with sticky wages and prices, wage and price indexation, capital accumulation, capital utilization, investment adjustment costs, government spending, habit in household consumption, and bond and cost-push shocks. I use this model in A Systematic Analysis of NGDP Targeting.

Model in Sims and Wu (2021)

I derive the model in Sims and Wu (2021). This model includes the aspects of the medium scale model above and adds financial intermediaries.
